## 5. Implementation Pathway

### Phase 1: Technical Validation (6–12 months)

**Site Selection Criteria:**
- Water stress index >0.4 (World Resources Institute Aqueduct) [27]
- Agricultural/aquaculture facilities within 5km radius
- Regulatory environment supporting resource recovery

**Pilot Success Metrics:**
- Condensate recovery: >30% of theoretical potential
- Thermal utilization: >20% of waste heat capacity
- Community engagement: Documented local partnership agreements

### Phase 2: Engineering Development (12–24 months)

- HVAC integration for condensate harvest systems
- Thermal distribution infrastructure design
- Water treatment to agricultural standards compliance

### Phase 3: Commercial Integration (3–5 years)

- Customer sustainability requirement analysis
- Regulatory framework establishment
- Partnership model development with local communities
