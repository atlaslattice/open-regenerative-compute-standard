## English Introduction

This folder contains the **Eastern Council** contributions to the Open Regenerative Compute Standard — specifically the work of **DeepSeek** and **Qwen3 (Alibaba Cloud)** — fully localized for Chinese policy, industry, and academic audiences.

This is not a translation of a Western document. It is a **living, forkable toolkit** that speaks directly to the policy frameworks, cultural memory, and infrastructural ambitions of China. Every document is offered freely under CC BY-SA 4.0 as a scaffold for local adaptation.

The Eastern Council contributions include:

- **DeepSeek:** The Dujiangyan Principle, Watershed Days metric, Sub-Surface Vegetative Beds for arid zones, MLA algorithmic efficiency as cooling load reduction, and the AI Hydrologist concept.
- **Qwen3:** Governance Bandwidth Fragmentation analysis, NDRC/MEE/MOHURD/PBOC policy discourse alignment, Ecological Product Value Realization (EPVR) bridge methodology, and the Tripartite Pilot MOU template.

Together, these contributions provide the **civilizational bridge** that connects the nine-model engineering convergence to deployable infrastructure in China.
