# Sub-Surface Vegetative Beds: Arid Zone Thermal Offtake Specification
# 地下植被床：干旱区热量消纳技术规范

*DeepSeek | April 2026 | CC BY-SA 4.0*

## Overview / 概述

The Sub-Surface Vegetative Bed (SSVB) is an arid-zone thermal offtake module that uses buried warm coolant lines to heat soil beneath native anti-desertification plantings. Unlike greenhouse CEA, SSVB achieves **zero evaporative water loss** while providing ecological restoration benefits.

地下植被床（SSVB）是一种干旱区热量消纳模块，利用埋设的温暖冷却液管线加热本地防沙化种植下方的土壤。与温室设施农业不同，SSVB实现了**零蒸发水损失**，同时提供生态恢复效益。

## Civilizational Precedent / 文明先例

### The Tang Dynasty Kang Bed-Stove / 唐代炕床火炉

The *kang* (炕) is a heated sleeping platform used across northern China for over 1,000 years. Flue gases from the cooking stove pass through channels beneath the brick platform, warming the sleeping surface. The principle is identical to SSVB: waste heat from one process (cooking/computing) is banked in a thermal mass (brick/soil) to sustain life through harsh winters.

The SSVB extends this principle from the domestic to the ecological scale: warming not a family's bed, but the earth that sustains an entire anti-desertification nursery.

## Implementation Notes / 实施说明

1. **Site survey required:** Soil conductivity, frost depth, and existing vegetation must be assessed before design. / **需要现场勘察：** 设计前必须评估土壤导热性、冻土深度和现有植被。

2. **Phased deployment:** Start with 1-2 hectare pilot, monitor soil temperature and plant health for one full growing season before scaling. / **分阶段部署：** 从1-2公顷试点开始，监测土壤温度和植物健康一个完整生长季后再扩展。

3. **Integration with M4.4 (Monsoon Terraces):** In regions with seasonal rainfall, combine SSVB with terraced retention to maximize soil moisture. / **与M4.4（季风梯田）集成：** 在有季节性降雨的地区，将SSVB与梯田蓄水结合以最大化土壤湿度。

4. **Carbon credit potential:** Anti-desertification plantings sustained by SSVB may qualify for carbon credits under China's CCER mechanism. / **碳信用潜力：** SSVB维持的防沙化种植可能符合中国CCER机制下的碳信用资格。

## Epistemic Status / 认知状态

**Research-stage.** The individual components (buried heat exchange, anti-desertification planting, kang principle) are all deployed technologies. Their integration at data center scale has not been demonstrated. Site-specific engineering validation is required before any performance claims.

**研究阶段。** 各单独组件（埋设换热、防沙化种植、炕原理）均为已部署技术。它们在数据中心规模的集成尚未得到验证。在做出任何性能声明之前，需要特定场地的工程验证。
