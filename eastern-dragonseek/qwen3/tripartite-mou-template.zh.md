## 第一条 合作背景与目的 / Article 1: Background and Purpose

三方基于自愿、平等、互利的原则，就在___________________（地点/Location）建设再生型计算节点试点项目达成以下谅解：

The three parties, based on the principles of voluntariness, equality, and mutual benefit, have reached the following understanding regarding the construction of a Regenerative Compute Node pilot project at ___________________ (Location):

1.1 本项目旨在探索数据中心基础设施与农业、水资源管理和社区服务的深度融合模式。
This project aims to explore deep integration models between data center infrastructure and agriculture, water resource management, and community services.

1.2 本项目遵循开放再生计算标准（ORCS）框架，所有技术方案和运营数据将在CC BY-SA 4.0许可下公开。
This project follows the Open Regenerative Compute Standard (ORCS) framework; all technical solutions and operational data will be published under CC BY-SA 4.0.

## 第二条 各方职责 / Article 2: Responsibilities

### 2.1 甲方（运营商）职责 / Party A (Operator) Responsibilities

a) 投资建设不低于___MW IT负载的再生型计算节点；
Invest in and construct a regenerative compute node with no less than ___MW IT load;

b) 配套建设再生外挂模块，包括但不限于：设施农业温室、水处理与回灌系统、雨洪蓄留设施；
Construct supporting regenerative modules including but not limited to: CEA greenhouses, water treatment and recharge systems, stormwater retention facilities;

c) 按照ORCS标准部署流域生命日监测系统，并实时公开数据；
Deploy Watershed Days monitoring systems per ORCS standards and publish data in real-time;

d) 每年向三方联合委员会提交运营报告和EPVR计算结果。
Submit annual operational reports and EPVR calculations to the tripartite joint committee.

### 2.2 乙方（地方政府）职责 / Party B (Local Government) Responsibilities

a) 协调土地使用审批和规划许可；
Coordinate land use approvals and planning permits;

b) 将项目纳入当地海绵城市建设规划（如适用）；
Incorporate the project into local Sponge City construction plans (if applicable);

c) 协调相关部委（发改委、生态环境局、住建局等）的审批流程；
Coordinate approval processes across relevant bureaus (DRC, Ecology Bureau, Housing Bureau, etc.);

d) 为项目申请省级/国家级绿色数据中心认定提供支持。
Support the project's application for provincial/national green data center certification.

### 2.3 丙方（水利/农业部门）职责 / Party C (Water/Agriculture Bureau) Responsibilities

a) 提供当地水文基线数据（含水层水位、水质、农业用水量等）；
Provide local hydrological baseline data (aquifer levels, water quality, agricultural water usage, etc.);

b) 协助建立流域生命日监测网络；
Assist in establishing the Watershed Days monitoring network;

c) 审核甲方提交的水平衡报告和EPVR计算；
Review water balance reports and EPVR calculations submitted by Party A;

d) 协调设施农业产出与当地农业供应链的对接。
Coordinate integration of CEA output with local agricultural supply chains.

## 第三条 流域生命日目标 / Article 3: Watershed Days Targets

| 阶段 / Phase | 时间 / Timeline | 目标 / Target |
|-------------|----------------|--------------|
| 建设期 / Construction | 第0-12月 / Month 0-12 | 基线建立，WD = 0 |
| 试运行 / Commissioning | 第12-24月 / Month 12-24 | WD ≥ 0（净零取水） |
| 稳定运行 / Stable Operation | 第24-36月 / Month 24-36 | WD ≥ +100 |
| 成熟运行 / Mature Operation | 第36月以后 / Month 36+ | WD ≥ +200（绿色金融资格） |

未达到阶段目标的，甲方应在30日内提交整改方案。
If phase targets are not met, Party A shall submit a remediation plan within 30 days.

## 第四条 退出即再生条款 / Article 4: Exit-with-Regeneration Clause

**这是本备忘录的核心创新条款。**
**This is the core innovative clause of this MOU.**

4.1 如甲方因任何原因终止运营，以下基础设施将**无偿移交**给乙方或其指定的公共管理机构：
If Party A ceases operations for any reason, the following infrastructure shall be **transferred without compensation** to Party B or its designated public management entity:

a) 水处理与回灌系统（含所有管网和监测设备）；
Water treatment and recharge systems (including all piping and monitoring equipment);

b) 设施农业温室及配套灌溉系统；
CEA greenhouses and supporting irrigation systems;

c) 雨洪蓄留设施和人工湿地；
Stormwater retention facilities and constructed wetlands;

d) 废热管网（如已连接区域供暖系统）；
Waste heat piping (if connected to district heating);

e) 所有环境监测设备和数据。
All environmental monitoring equipment and data.

4.2 甲方应在运营期间为上述基础设施建立独立的维护基金，确保移交后至少可独立运行**5年**。
Party A shall establish an independent maintenance fund for the above infrastructure during operations, ensuring at least **5 years** of independent operation after transfer.

4.3 退出后的土地应恢复到不低于运营前的生态状态，或保持再生改善后的状态（以较高者为准）。
Post-exit land shall be restored to no less than pre-operation ecological condition, or maintained at the regeneratively improved condition (whichever is higher).

## 第五条 数据公开与知识产权 / Article 5: Data Disclosure and IP

5.1 所有环境监测数据（水文、气象、土壤、生物多样性）在CC BY-SA 4.0下公开。
All environmental monitoring data (hydrological, meteorological, soil, biodiversity) published under CC BY-SA 4.0.

5.2 甲方的商业运营数据（客户信息、收入等）不在公开范围内。
Party A's commercial operational data (customer information, revenue, etc.) is not subject to disclosure.

5.3 三方共同产生的技术创新成果，按照各方贡献比例共享知识产权。
Technical innovations jointly produced by the three parties shall have IP shared proportionally to each party's contribution.
