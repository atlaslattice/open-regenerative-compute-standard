### 🌊 什么是 ORCS？| What is ORCS?

**ZH**: 开放再生计算标准（ORCS）是一份模块化、开源的超大规模数据中心再生基础设施技术清单，共包含46项已部署或可部署的技术模块。该标准源于九大人工智能系统的跨大陆共识：*再生农业原则可直接应用于规模化计算*。

**EN**: The Open Regenerative Compute Standard (ORCS) is a modular, open-source inventory of 46 deployed or deployable regenerative infrastructure technologies for hyperscale data centers. It emerged from the convergence of nine independent AI systems across three continents: *regenerative agriculture principles apply directly to computing at scale*.
